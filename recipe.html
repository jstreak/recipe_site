Chocolate Cake

This chocokate cake recipe is a classic.This version of the recipe is fast and quick and your kids will love it!

DifficultyPreparationCookingTotal Time
Easy10 min25 min35 min

Ingredients

Black chocolate: 200 g

Sugar: 150 grams

Butter: 200 grams

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80 grams

Instructions

1

Melt the chocolate and the butter together

2

Mix the sugar, eggs, and the flour

3

Mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

Bake it in the oven
